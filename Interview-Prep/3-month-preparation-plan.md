## Pro Tips

1. **Consistency over Intensity:** 2-3 hours daily is better than 10 hours on Sunday
2. **Understand, Don't Memorize:** Focus on patterns, not solutions
3. **Write Code on Paper:** Helps in actual interviews
4. **Explain Your Approach:** Practice thinking out loud
5. **Time Yourself:** Simulate real interview pressure
6. **Review Mistakes:** Maintain an error log
7. **Stay Healthy:** Sleep, exercise, mental health matter
8. **Join Communities:** Reddit (r/cscareerquestions), Discord, Telegram groups
9. **Network:** Attend meetups, connect with people in target companies
10. **Don't Give Up:** Rejections are part of the process
